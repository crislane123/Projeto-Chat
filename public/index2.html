Security By Default

Some of your most personal moments are shared on Grifinória chat, which is why we built end-to-end encryption into the latest versions of our app. When end-to-end encrypted, your messages and calls are secured so only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to them, and nobody in between, not even Grifinória chat.
